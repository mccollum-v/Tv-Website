Will Trent ABC's Police Drama

Roman Rodriguez plays Will Trent and this is my new favorite shows. Will has dyslexia but he is the best agent in the office.

Sonja Sohn aka Amanda is the head of the Georgia Bureau of Investigation and Trent's (Ramón Rodríguez) boss.

When an APD agent goes missing, Will examines the crime scene and locates a secret message putting him back on the path to justice; with his lineage in question, Will realizes the answers he seeks have been in front of him all along.
